Workshop Rules for Participants

Welcome to the workshop! To ensure a fun and productive experience for everyone, please follow these rules:

  • Respect Others: Treat everyone – instructors, fellow participants, and staff with kindness and respect. Listen when others are speaking and be supportive.
  • Be On Time: Arrive on time for each session. If you're going to be late or absent, inform the instructor ahead of time. (Important Reminder: Consistent participation is important for completing the 18-week program successfully.)
  • Follow Safety Instructions: Always follow the safety guidelines provided. If you're unsure about something, ask for help.
  • Take Care of Workshop Materials: Respect and properly use all equipment and materials. Return everything to its place after use.
  • Stay Focused: Keep distractions like phones, online games away during activities.
  • Participate Fully: Engage in all activities and discussions. Your input is valuable, and we want everyone to contribute.
  • Be Responsible: Take responsibility for your actions and behavior during the workshop. Clean up after yourself and respect the space.
  • Ask Questions: Don’t hesitate to ask questions or request help if you need it. We’re here to support you. Communicate in English or in German language. Don't feel shy to fail!
  • Maintain a Positive Attitude: Approach each session with a positive attitude. Learning is more fun when you’re open to new ideas and experiences
  • Have Fun: Enjoy the process, be creative, and have fun learning with your peers.
  • Do you promise to follow all the rules and make the best of this opportunity to learn here?
Failure to follow the rules may result in the following consequences:
  • 30 minutes suspension.
  • 1 day suspension.
  • Complete suspension
