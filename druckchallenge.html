Learning Goals

  • Create awareness of everyday objects
  • Create shapes and objects
  • Develop and implement own ideas (No downloading and copying from internet)

  • Promote creativity

Things needed for the challenge


SD card


Filament


Laptop and mouse


Tinkercad
