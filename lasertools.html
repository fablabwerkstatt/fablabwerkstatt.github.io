Tools used for designing

Do remember and keep in mind what are you designing. Because for lasering we are using 2D design. You can draw a design on paper and trace it using any CAD or illustrator tools. Convert the design to be engrave or cut in our laser machines.

For vector design
  • Adobe illustrator
  • Inkscape
  • CorelDraw
  • Autodesk Fusion360
For Raster design
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • GIMP
  • Corel PaintShop Pro
  • MS Paint
