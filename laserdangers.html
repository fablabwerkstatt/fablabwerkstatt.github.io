DANGERS of WORKING with Laser Machines!

It is very important to follow certain rules while working with laser machines.

Fire

  • The base material is vaporized, generating significant heat
  • Some materials may catch fire during cutting
  • Dirt and deposits can cause fires
  • Be Cautious and take a help of your teacher in laser room without any shame!

Dangerous fumes and exhaust gases

  • When cutting certain materials, laser-generated airborne contaminants (LGACs) can be produced (health risk!)
  • Examples: Benzene, toluene, hydrochloric acid, isocyanates
  • Exhaust systems and/or filter systems are required to vent air outside
  • They must be properly installed and meet all manufacturer specifications
  • Particle contamination

Additional Risks

  • Eye damage from direct laser radiation or scattered radiation (visible and invisible!).
  • Loss of vision.
  • Irreparable damage.
  • Damage to skin and other tissue types.
  • Sharp edges (cutting injuries).
  • Electrical hazard Crushing danger.
