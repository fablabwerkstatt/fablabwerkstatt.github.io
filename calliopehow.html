How to Program Calliope mini?

Open Roberta Lab

Go to Open Roberta Lab there you can change language on the top. Create your account. Good thing you do not need password.

Register in Open Roberta Lab

To Sign in must register your account. If you are below 16 year do not worry about signing in with email address. You must remember your Username and Password as it will be used for sign in later.

Open Calliope Mini

On the main screen of the Open Roberat Lab page locate Calliope mini and click on Get Started. It will take you to page with "Start" and some colorful function on the left hand and right hand side is important.

Our First program

We can do first sample program blinking LED light. Follow the video please.

Drag blocks from the side bars and join them with the start. Test the program in Simulation and download it.

Load program in Calliope mini

Open your calliope mini from box and take out Calliope and USB cable. Follow the video for instructions.

Copy the file in your download folder/local drive and paste it in MINI drive to run the program. And see the LED lights blinking.
